Frequently Asked Questions

Is this for beginners?

Absolutely. The Business Suite is designed to take you from idea to launch and beyond, no matter your starting point. We cover the fundamentals in a clear, actionable way.

Do I need a business idea to start?

No. The first module of the Solopreneur Launchpad is designed to help you find and validate a business idea that aligns with your skills and passions.

What if I already have a business?

The Business Suite is still for you. The Capital Suite will teach you how to build business credit and access funding to grow your existing business, and the Solopreneur Launchpad can help you refine your model and strategy.
