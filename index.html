Our Three Divisions

Each division serves a unique purpose in our mission to create transparent, supportive communities where everyone can thrive.

✓ Verified Veteran

Digital Grandpa

STORYTELLING & MENTORSHIP

Connect with wisdom keepers who've walked difficult paths. Share your story, find mentorship, and help others navigate their journeys through the power of lived experience.

What You'll Get:

  • One-on-one mentorship connections
  • Weekly community story circles
  • Platform to share your wisdom
  • Legacy building support
  • Intergenerational healing opportunities

How to Join Digital Grandpa:

  1. 1Complete our story intake form
  2. 2Schedule a welcome call with Bob
  3. 3Attend your first community circle
  4. 4Begin sharing or receiving mentorship

Rise & Reclaim

COMMUNITY & RECOVERY

Join a supportive community of individuals in recovery building meaningful lives through shared work, mutual support, and transparent income opportunities.

What You'll Get:

  • Weekly recovery support groups
  • Paid work opportunities (50/50 revenue splits)
  • Skills training and development
  • 24/7 peer support network
  • Financial transparency and fair compensation

How to Join Rise & Reclaim:

  1. 1Complete recovery status assessment
  2. 2Attend a virtual community meeting
  3. 3Complete skills and interests survey
  4. 4Begin with community support activities

Beacon Labs

TOOLS & SYSTEMS

Access cutting-edge tools for transparent community building, automated revenue sharing, and impact tracking. Perfect for organizations and community leaders.

What You'll Get:

  • Automated revenue splitting tools
  • Real-time impact dashboards
  • Community management platform
  • Technical support and training
  • Custom integration assistance

How to Access Beacon Labs:

  1. 1Schedule a platform demonstration
  2. 2Discuss your community's needs
  3. 3Receive custom implementation plan
  4. 4Begin with guided setup and training
