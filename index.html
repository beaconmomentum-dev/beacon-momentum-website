Why Beacon Exists

The economic reality for millions of Americans has become increasingly harsh. The system that once promised opportunity now creates barriers. Beacon was built to address these realities head-on.

  • Housing Crisis: The median home price has increased 400% since 1980, while wages have barely kept pace with inflation. Homeownership is now out of reach for most young Americans.
  • Wage Stagnation: Real wages have remained flat for 40 years while productivity has soared. Workers are producing more value but seeing less of it.
  • Debt Traps: Student loans, medical debt, and credit card debt have created a permanent underclass unable to build wealth or achieve financial security.
  • Retirement Crisis: Pensions have disappeared, 401(k)s are underfunded, and Social Security is uncertain. Most Americans will never be able to retire.
  • Generational Theft: Millennials and Gen Z are the first generations expected to be poorer than their parents, despite being more educated and working longer hours.
  • System Failure: The traditional path (college ‚Üí job ‚Üí house ‚Üí retirement) no longer works for the majority. New pathways are required.
