The Technology

The Autonomous Business Stack

Four layers of technology, working together. Each layer is production-tested inside the Beacon Momentum ecosystem before we deploy it for clients.

Layer 1 — Orchestration

n8n Workflow Engine

The backbone of every automation. Connects your existing tools (CRM, email, Slack, databases) and routes tasks between AI agents without requiring code changes to your current systems.

Layer 2 — Reasoning

LangChain / CrewAI Agents

Where the intelligence lives. Agents that can reason through multi-step problems, delegate to sub-agents, and adapt to new information — not just follow rigid if-then rules.

Layer 3 — Security

NemoClaw Governance

NVIDIA's enterprise security wrapper for AI agents. Role-based access controls, sandboxed execution, and full audit logs. Every agent action is logged, attributable, and reversible.

Layer 4 — Delivery

Cloudflare AI Gateway

Secure, cost-controlled routing for all AI API calls. Rate limiting, data governance, and cost monitoring ensure client data never touches unauthorized infrastructure.

The VSO Technology Partner Program

Beacon Labs is actively seeking 501(c)(3) veteran service organizations to partner with on VA-funded grant programs. We provide the technology and peer-support delivery platform; you provide the grant management infrastructure.

Why Partner With Beacon Labs?

The VA's SSG Fox Suicide Prevention Grant Program explicitly prioritizes "nontraditional and innovative approaches" to reaching veterans who are not currently in VA care. That is exactly what Beacon Labs delivers — a digital-first, AI-enhanced peer support and economic stabilization platform that reaches the 60% of at-risk veterans who never walk through a VA door.

We are a Wyoming LLC actively seeking a 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsor for the June 12, 2026 Fox Grant application ($100,000–$750,000 award range). The partnership model is straightforward: you manage grant compliance and receive a standard administrative fee; Beacon Labs delivers the program and the technology.

1

Discovery Call

30-minute conversation to confirm mission alignment and grant eligibility.

2

MOU Execution

Simple Memorandum of Understanding defining the financial split and responsibilities.

3

Joint Application

We write the program narrative; you submit as the primary applicant before June 12.

4

Program Delivery

Beacon Labs deploys the platform and delivers measurable outcomes for your reporting.

Ideal Partner Profile

Current or previous Fox Grant recipient • Mission focused on veteran suicide prevention, transition support, or economic stabilization • Serving populations not currently engaged with VA healthcare • Open to technology-enhanced delivery models
