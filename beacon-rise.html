Beacon Rise: The Path Forward

Beacon Rise is a 12-week, structured journey designed to give you the tools, community, and guidance to rebuild your life from the ground up. It's not about quick fixes or empty platitudes. It's about doing the real work of healing and growth, surrounded by people who get it because they've lived it.

Rooted in the Digital Grandpa philosophy of wisdom, compassion, and practical action, this program will help you find your footing, rediscover your purpose, and build a future that is authentically yours. This is where you stop surviving and start thriving.

The 12-Week Journey to a New You

Weeks 1-4: The Foundation

We start by taking stock and building a solid foundation for your new life. This is about radical honesty, self-assessment, and understanding the patterns that have led you to this point.

Weeks 5-8: The Rebuilding

With a clear understanding of your past, we move into the rebuilding phase. This is where you'll develop new skills, new habits, and a new mindset. We'll focus on resilience, emotional intelligence, and practical life design.

Weeks 9-12: The Launch

In the final phase, we turn our attention to the future. You'll create a personal roadmap, set meaningful goals, and take the first steps into your new life with confidence and a supportive community at your back.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program only for veterans?

No. While Beacon Rise was inspired by the challenges faced by veterans, it is open to anyone who is navigating a significant life transition and is ready to do the work of rebuilding.

What if I can't make a live session?

All live sessions are recorded and will be available to watch at your convenience. You won't miss a thing.

Is this therapy?

Beacon Rise is a peer-support and coaching program, not a substitute for professional therapy. If you are in need of clinical mental health support, we encourage you to seek the help of a licensed therapist.

What is the time commitment?

Expect to spend 3-5 hours per week on the program, including the weekly module, group coaching call, and community interaction.
