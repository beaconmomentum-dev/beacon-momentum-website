Our 15-30 Day Money-Back Guarantee

We are confident that the Beacon Momentum Founding Member program will provide you with immense value. However, we want you to feel completely secure in your investment. That's why we offer a conditional 15-30 day money-back guarantee.

15-Day "No Questions Asked" Refund

If for any reason you are not satisfied with the program within the first 15 days of your purchase, you may request a full refund, no questions asked.

30-Day "Show Your Work" Refund

If you are between 16 and 30 days of your purchase, we offer a refund conditional on you demonstrating that you have engaged with the material. To be eligible for a refund during this period, you must show that you have:

  1. Completed at least 3 modules from any of the pathways (Rise & Reclaim, Solopreneur Launchpad, or Capital Suite).
  2. Participated in at least one community discussion or Q&A session.
  3. Made a genuine effort to apply the strategies and concepts taught in the program.

Why this policy? We believe this policy is fair and encourages you to give the program a real try. We are here to support you, and if you do the work and still don't see the value, we will gladly refund your investment.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at support@beaconmomentum.com with the subject line "Refund Request." Please include your name, email address, and date of purchase.

If you are requesting a refund between days 16 and 30, please also include evidence of your work as outlined above. This can be screenshots of completed modules, links to your community posts, or a brief summary of how you've applied the concepts.

Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days. The funds will be returned to your original method of payment.
