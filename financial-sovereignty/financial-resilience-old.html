Stage 1: The Ashes (Survival)

Your Focus: Stabilize. Learn basic budgeting. Understand where money goes. Create breathing room.

Stage 2: The Stirring (Stability)

Your Focus: Build emergency savings. Reduce debt. Learn about credit and financial tools. Create margin.

Stage 3: The Rising (Growth)

You have breathing room. Emergency fund exists. Debt is manageable or eliminated. You're thinking beyond survival to building something.

Your Focus: Develop income skills. Explore value creation. Learn about assets and wealth building. Expand possibilities.

Stage 4: The Soaring (Sovereignty)

You make financial decisions from abundance, not scarcity. You understand systems. You have options. Money serves your goals rather than controlling your choices.

Your Focus: Optimize strategies. Explore advanced concepts. Give back. Help others on their journey.

Progress isn't linear. You might move between stages. That's normal. What matters is the direction of your trajectory and the skills you're building along the way.
