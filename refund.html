Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

We stand behind our programs. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are available for Beacon Momentum programs, services, and memberships. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. General Refund Policy

Beacon Momentum offers refunds under specific circumstances as outlined below. All refund requests are subject to review and approval. We reserve the right to deny refunds that do not meet the criteria specified in this policy.

Important: By purchasing any Beacon Momentum program or service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund Policy. All sales are considered final unless you qualify for a refund under the terms described below.

2. Program-Specific Refund Terms

Refund eligibility varies by program type. Please review the terms applicable to your purchase.

2.1 Educational Programs (Beacon Rise, Beacon Launch, Beacon Academy)

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for our educational programs. If you are not satisfied with the program, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase date, provided you meet the following conditions:

  • You have completed less than 25% of the program content
  • You have not downloaded or accessed more than 25% of course materials
  • You submit a refund request in writing to support@beaconmomentum.com
  • You provide brief feedback explaining why the program was not a good fit

After 30 Days: Refunds are not available after 30 days from the date of purchase, except in cases of technical issues that prevent access to the program and cannot be resolved by our support team.

2.2 Mentorship Services (Digital Grandpa)

First-Session Satisfaction Guarantee: If you are not satisfied after your first mentorship session, you may request a full refund within 14 days of that session. To qualify:

  • You must have attended the first scheduled session
  • You must submit a refund request within 14 days of the first session
  • You have not scheduled or attended additional sessions

Monthly Subscriptions: For monthly mentorship subscriptions, you may cancel at any time, but refunds are not provided for partial months. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

After First Session: Once you have attended more than one session or the 14-day window has passed, refunds are not available. You may cancel your subscription to prevent future charges, but no refund will be issued for the current billing period.

2.3 Done-For-You Technology Services (Beacon Labs)

Milestone-Based Refunds: For done-for-you technology services, refunds are calculated based on project milestones:

  • Before work begins: Full refund minus a 10% administrative fee
  • After initial discovery/planning (0-25% complete): 75% refund
  • After development begins (25-50% complete): 50% refund
  • After 50% completion: No refund available

Completion percentage is determined by Beacon Labs based on project scope, deliverables completed, and time invested.

2.4 Community Memberships

Monthly Memberships: You may cancel your membership at any time. No refunds are provided for partial months, but your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Annual Memberships: Annual memberships are eligible for a prorated refund within the first 60 days. After 60 days, no refunds are available, but you may cancel to prevent auto-renewal.

2.5 Founding Member Lifetime Access

Limited Refund Window: Founding Member lifetime access purchases are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase, provided you have not accessed more than 10% of available content or attended any live events.

After 14 Days: Founding Member purchases are non-refundable after 14 days due to the significant discount and lifetime access provided.

3. Non-Refundable Items and Services

The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • One-time consultation or coaching sessions (after they have occurred)
  • Live event tickets or workshop registrations (within 14 days of the event)
  • Digital downloads or materials that have been accessed or downloaded
  • Certification fees or exam costs
  • Third-party services or tools purchased through Beacon Momentum
  • Custom work or deliverables that have been completed and delivered

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Support: Email support@beaconmomentum.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Provide Details: Include your name, email address, order number, purchase date, and the program or service for which you are requesting a refund
  3. Explain Reason: Briefly explain why you are requesting a refund (this helps us improve our services)
  4. Wait for Review: Our team will review your request within 3-5 business days
  5. Receive Decision: You will receive an email confirming approval or denial of your refund request

5. Refund Processing

Once your refund is approved:

  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • Bank Processing: Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed

6. Subscription Cancellations

For subscription-based services (monthly mentorship, community memberships, technology services):

  • How to Cancel: Log into your account and navigate to "Subscription Settings," or email support@beaconmomentum.com
  • Effective Date: Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No Partial Refunds: You will not receive a refund for the remaining days in your current billing period
  • Access: You will retain access to the service until the end of the paid period
  • Reactivation: You may reactivate your subscription at any time by logging into your account

7. Technical Issues and Access Problems

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from accessing purchased content or services:

  • Contact Support First: Email support@beaconmomentum.com to report the issue
  • Troubleshooting: Our team will work with you to resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • Refund Eligibility: If we cannot resolve the issue within 14 days, you will be eligible for a full refund

8. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or credit card company without first contacting us:

  • Your account will be immediately suspended
  • You will lose access to all Beacon Momentum services and content
  • We reserve the right to pursue legal action to recover costs associated with the chargeback
  • You will be permanently banned from future purchases

Please contact us first if you have concerns about a charge. We are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.

9. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. If you believe you have a unique situation that warrants consideration for a refund outside of this policy, please contact us at support@beaconmomentum.com. We will review your case on an individual basis.

Examples of circumstances we may consider:

  • Serious illness or medical emergency
  • Death of the purchaser
  • Military deployment
  • Natural disaster or force majeure event

Documentation may be required to support your request.

10. Refund Policy for International Customers

This refund policy applies to customers worldwide. However, please note:

  • Refunds are issued in U.S. Dollars (USD)
  • Currency conversion fees charged by your bank are not refundable
  • International transaction fees are not refundable
  • Refund processing times may vary depending on your country and payment method

11. Changes to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your purchase of any Beacon Momentum service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

The refund policy in effect at the time of your purchase will govern your refund eligibility for that specific purchase.
